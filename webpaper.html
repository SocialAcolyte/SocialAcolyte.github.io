· How it's free

Bring your own Claude.

No servers, no subscription, no API key. It runs on the Claude account you already chat with.

Your login, borrowedSigned in to claude.ai? webpaper uses that session. New to Claude? The free tier works.
Never charged by usWe have no plan and never see your card. Any AI work counts against your own Claude, like a normal message.
Nothing leaves your browserNo telemetry, no account on our side. The page, your prompt, and your Claude are the whole loop.
· Install · beta 0.0.1

Set up in a minute.

It is not on the Chrome Web Store yet, so you add it by hand. No coding, no command line.

Download webpaper.zip

You need: a Chromium browser (Chrome, Edge, Brave, Arc) and a Claude account. It is free at claude.ai.

  1. 1Sign in to Claude. Open claude.ai and log in. The free plan works.
  2. 2Download and unzip the file. Keep the webpaper folder somewhere you won't delete.
  3. 3Open chrome://extensions. Turn on Developer mode, top right.
  4. 4Click Load unpacked. Choose the webpaper folder. The cat appears in your toolbar.
  5. 5Click the cat, allow Claude, switch it on, and open any page.
