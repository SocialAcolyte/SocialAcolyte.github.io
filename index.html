I build the datasets that decide what frontier models can and cannot do. Most of the gap between what AI promises and what it delivers lives inside the data -- I want to close it.

philosophy

Frontier capability is bottlenecked by data, not compute. The next leap won't be a new architecture -- it will be data designed by people who knew exactly what was missing. I build that data, and I build it as an acolyte: a disciple who studies the work hard enough to deserve to lead it.

Like the sparse, 96% empty space of a silica tile that insulates a spacecraft from re-entry plasma, dataset curation is the art of strategic omission. We build the sparse, high-fidelity matrices that insulate model boundaries from behavioral entropy.

Molecular Curation // Silica Fiber Matrix

Mechanical illustration of thermal dissipation within a Fused Silica tile (LI-900). Your cursor acts as a localized plasma heat vector. As you glide across the boundary layer (top), it injects high-density kinetic thermal particles. These particles diffuse downwards, colliding, deflecting, and generating molecular collision sparks upon striking the glass fiber lattice, which vibrates in response. The heat stays localized at the re-entry boundary, leaving the structural aluminum skin (bottom) secure.
