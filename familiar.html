what familiar does

01 rewrites every page

three layers of CSS land before you blink. typography, color, layout. then Claude studies the page structure and redesigns it. then it cross-references your memory and weights what matters to you.

02 knows who you are

you write a short document about yourself once. familiar carries that context everywhere. every surface of the internet starts adjusting to your presence.

03 briefs every AI

when you open Claude, ChatGPT, or Grok, your profile is already in the context. no re-explaining yourself. the AI knows you before you type a word.

install / early build

Download familiar.zip
  1. 01 Unzip the downloaded file. Keep the folder somewhere permanent. Chrome loads from it directly.
  2. 02 Open chrome://extensions and enable Developer mode (top-right toggle).
  3. 03 Click Load unpacked and select the unzipped folder.
  4. 04 Pin familiar from the extensions menu. Click it and grant claude.ai access when prompted.
  5. 05 Write a few lines about yourself in the Memory tab. Browse. Pink marks are strong matches, blue are secondary.

requires a Claude account. free tier works. familiar borrows your existing session and never stores your credentials.
